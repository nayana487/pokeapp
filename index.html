What Do Pokemon Look Like?

Oh no, the Pokedek has been hacked! All Pokemon photos have been replaced by images of chicken tenders. No worries, this app has your back. Click on the name of a Pokemon to see what they look like as you continue on your quest. Gotta catch 'em all!

Disclaimer: The creator of this app only acknowledges the existence of the original 150 pokemon. Unfortunately, the pokeapi doesn't let you filter out the posers. Though I probably could have chopped the list up myself in the js file.
